PX Crossposter

Privacy and Terms

Crossposter is a private self-hosted publishing tool for manually posting content to social accounts controlled by the operator.

Effective date: June 2, 2026

About

Crossposter is not a public social network, agency service, or ad platform. It is used to prepare posts, attach media, and publish to connected social accounts when the operator clicks publish.

Source code: github.com/apoorvdarshan/crossposter

Privacy

  • No analytics, advertising pixels, or tracking cookies are used by this page.
  • Crossposter stores social API credentials only in the operator's own local or self-hosted configuration.
  • Credentials are used only to publish content the operator submits manually.
  • Uploaded media and draft text are used only for publishing and local history.
  • Crossposter does not sell personal information or share data with third parties except the selected social platforms during publishing.

Terms

  • Use Crossposter only with accounts, boards, pages, and profiles you own or are authorized to manage.
  • You are responsible for the content you publish and for following each platform's rules.
  • The software is provided without warranty.
  • Access may be removed or disabled at any time for security, abuse prevention, or platform policy reasons.
